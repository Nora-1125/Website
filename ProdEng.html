Production Engineering Course 2025

The course that started this page! Read about what I'm learning in this course and take a look at my projects.

What is this course?

The Enginnering Science curriculum doesn't include classical production engineering as one might expect in Mechanical Engineering. But we can take this elective course to still aquire some understanding of the topic.

Given the freedom of being an elective course, the theory is accompanied by a whole lot of practical tasks and space for own ideas and projects.
