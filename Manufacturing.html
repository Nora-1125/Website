Manufacturing Methods

Preparation for the final project

Text

After being given a theoretical overview of the manufacturing method of masterforming for two sessions of the course, it was time for more practical things.

At first a BZT PFE 1510PX CNC machine was demonstrated, along with a brief explanation of G-Code, the programming language used for CNC machines. Next a hands-on introduction to a Trotec laser cutter, a Proxxon lathe and a Proxxon milling machine followed, qualifiyng us to use these machines on our own. Finally we were shown plastic being cast into a UV 3D-printed mold, were the importance of high quality molds became forcefully clear, due to the cast failing because of a to badly worn mold.

Additionally, a short introduction to Inkshttps://inkscape.org/cape was given for the creation of our own design for the laser cutter.
