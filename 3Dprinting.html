3D-Printing Projects

Some Small Projects taking first steps with cad and 3d-printing

Mobile Phone Stand

Guided by a lecturer, a simple mobile phone stand design was recreated using AUTODESK Fusion. This introduced some basic sketching and construction operations and gave a rough overview over the controls.

After the model was done, a brief introduction to 3D-printing in general and PrusaSlicer was given. The stand was then printed on a Prusa MK4.

Pencil Holder

Similar to the Mobile Phone Stand this is a design recreated according to a set of instructions. It helped discovering further operations in and functions of AUTODESK Fusion, wich provided some further preparation for the final project.

The finished design was already silced using PrusaSlicer, but not yet printed.

Please go visit the Robo Dog Project Page and take a look at Head and Tail, Feet and Shanks or Main Body and Lid to see more of my CAD and 3D-printing work!
